Zac Morton is a Software Engineer and is born and raised in Southern California. He graduated with a BA in Communications from California State University, Fullerton. He is attending UCI's Full Stack Web Development where he is focusing on the MERN stack. His relationship with computers began at an early age. As the son of a physics and special education teacher, was lucky enough that his parents brought home a Macintosh Classic computer for the summer. Playing video games such as Odell Lake, The Oregon Trail, and Where in the World is Carmen San Diego developed a passion for learning and pixels.
In 2014, Zac founded, produced, and hosted the LoudGrowl.com Podcast. It was a series of shows built around sports, movies, and video games. LoudGrowl.com is reforming and is slated to restart. Look for it anywhere you get your podcasts in late-2019 or early-2020. Currently, he works as Lead Content Strategist with GlamourGraceFaith.com. Taking a fashion brand that had less than a thousand followers three years ago, and developing a steady growth trajectory to where it exists at over 14 thousand. He continues to work with major brands such as Lulu's, Vince Camuto, and other lifestyle brands. Contact for consultation on content development strategies.
Zac is passionate about education and volunteers working with young students and schools to foster an excitement towards technology and photography that was critical to him at a young age. Most recently, he volunteered for a fundraiser at St. Lucy's school taking pictures of Santa with the student body. In his free time, he is a giant sports fan of the Lakers and Dodgers and is sad that Game of Thrones is drawing to a close. He also loves snowboarding, visiting national parks, and keeping up with the latest in the world of technology and science.
